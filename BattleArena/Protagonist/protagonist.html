Terra Umbra

Abyssal Forge of Oblivion

"From the depths, we shape eternity."

Zizius’s followers, known as the Terra Umbra, are unwavering in their devotion to his principles. They seek to impose structure and hierarchy across the planes, often clashing with agents of chaos and rebellion. Temples to Zizius are grand citadels of light, their spires piercing the heavens as a symbol of his unrelenting vigilance.


Overview

The Terra Umbra, or "Earth’s Shadow," is a fanatical cult forged in the image of Zizius, embodying his unyielding pursuit of order and authority. Unlike other religious sects, the Terra Umbra views the multiverse as a flawed creation that must be reforged through discipline and destruction of chaos. They work tirelessly to reshape existence into a perfect reflection of Zizius’s divine will, even if it means obliterating entire civilizations to do so.


Role in the Cosmos

Terra Umbra is feared across realms, not just for their relentless pursuit of order but for the unyielding methods they employ. They believe their work is not cruel but necessary, a cosmic cleansing in service of the ultimate truth: only Zizius’s order can save existence from itself.

The cult is a relentless tide of discipline and destruction, and their name alone is enough to send chaos-aligned realms into disarray.

Greatest Adversary

Lore of Coming Soon

Coming Soon

Zizius’s greatest adversary is Aien, the embodiment of chaos and change. Their battles are legendary, each clash shaping the very fabric of reality. While Zizius seeks to contain Aien’s destructive tendencies, he often underestimates the necessity of change and adaptation, leading to moments where his rigid ideals threaten to unravel his vision.

Even within himself, Zizius harbors a quiet fear—what if his pursuit of order extinguishes the very essence of existence? This doubt fuels his determination but also makes him vulnerable to manipulation by those who recognize the cracks in his unyielding façade.


Relationships with His Siblings

Kiana

Creation and Renewal

"From the ashes of the old, I craft the new; life and death are threads in my tapestry."

Zizius respected Kiana’s creative abilities but saw her as overly sentimental and reckless in her pursuit of renewal. While he admired her craftsmanship, he condemned her belief in the cyclical balance of life and death, arguing that creation should be permanent and unchanging. Their debates often turned into bitter disputes, with Kiana accusing Zizius of being a heartless tyrant and Zizius chastising her for her "foolish naivety."

Aien

Chaos and Change

"I am the storm that shatters foundations; only through destruction can creation thrive."

Zizius viewed Aien as a direct threat to his ideals. Where Zizius built, Aien destroyed; where Zizius enforced order, Aien reveled in chaos. Their clashes were the most violent of all the siblings, with battles that shook the very fabric of existence. Zizius vowed to one day "break the storm" that Aien embodied, believing that his brother’s chaotic influence was the greatest flaw in Godthrone’s creation.

Xezphine

Knowledge and Mystery

"Truth lies veiled in shadows; seek me, and you may find enlightenment—or oblivion."

Xezphine’s enigmatic nature frustrated Zizius, who craved clarity and control. Though he valued her wisdom, he mistrusted her intentions, believing her to harbor secrets that could destabilize the order he sought to impose. Their relationship was a cautious one, marked by mutual respect but also a lingering tension. Xezphine, in turn, saw Zizius as blind to the complexities of existence, dismissing his rigid ideals as dangerous oversimplifications.
